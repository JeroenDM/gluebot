Gluebot App

First, launch the rosdriver for the UR5. The ip adress of the computer should be in the same range. Then launch the rviz and moveit setup. Now start the gluebot app. And finally refresh this page.


          roslaunch ur_bringup ur5_bringup.launch robot_ip:=192.168.1.5
          roslaunch gluebot_support setup.launch
          roslaunch gluebot_app start_app.launch
